Experience

simplybuy.it

since 05/2015, Founder, Developer

Simplybuy.it is the simplest checkout platform one can use.

I was always annoyed with shop platforms, so annoyed that I set forth to build my own. Simplybuy.it is the simplest approach to a common problem: Checkout a product on the internet. It allows sellers to simply create a link to a checkout page where customers can enter their shipping information. The checkout process is the shortest, best optimized checkout process you can find on the internet.

Opencorporates.com

since 03/2015, Developer

Opencorporates is the worlds largest open company database.

At Opencorporates I was working on their API and an internal reconciliation service. I have also worked on their flexible ETL pipeline that allows measured reconciliation on vague information. This allows their system to ingest and renconcile more data, from more source than they could have done before.

PaperC

02/2008 - 06/2011, Co-Founder, Developer

PaperC was my Start-Up company. I was one of the three founders and had the role of the CTO. Our idea was to virtualize textbooks and specialist literature and thusly making them accessible to everyone. During my time with PaperC I worked together with Upstream Berlin. My responsibilites as CTO went from implementing features to keeping the infrastructure healthy. The team size varied a lot since we often worked together with freelancers in our project teams. At its peak the team grew up to 6 people who worked on the platform.
